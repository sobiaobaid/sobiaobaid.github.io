Welcome!

I’m Sobia Obaid, a Computer Systems Engineer and a dedicated Educator

I earned my Bachelor's and Master of Engineering degrees from NED University of Engineering and Technology , graduating with distinction in both programs. My academic excellence was recognized with a gold medal, a testament to my commitment to the field.

Since beginning my teaching career at NEDUET in 2007 , I have accumulated extensive experience as a lecturer and academic mentor. Over the years, I have guided numerous students in the areas of computer science and software development.

In addition to my university-level teaching, I also offer A Level tuition for Computer Science, specializing in exam preparation and conceptual understanding.

I am passionate about helping students build a strong foundation in computer science and programming concepts to support their growth as confident and capable problem-solvers.

Besides my academic pursuits, I’m an art lover and enjoy painting landscapes and nature-inspired art.
